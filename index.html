Our Impact
Making a Difference

Since becoming a HUD National Intermediary in 2009, Nueva Esperanza has supported a robust network of nonprofit counseling agencies across the nation. We've assisted over 2 million homeowners through grant assessment, training, sub-awards, and technical assistance.

Our mission is to empower these agencies to serve their communities effectively, providing crucial services such as pre-purchase advice, credit repair, financial literacy, and foreclosure intervention.

Learn More
The Need
Why Housing Counseling Matters

Housing Counseling is vital in our communities. People need support with pre-purchase advice, credit repair, financial literacy, rental counseling, home repair programs, and protection from financial scams.

With our help, organizations can learn how to begin serving their neighborhoods and grow their impact. We provide the guidance needed to navigate HUD regulations and reporting requirements.

What it Takes
Requirements for Participation

To join our network, organizations must be a 501(c)(3) nonprofit in good standing, have administered a housing counseling program for at least one year, have a certified HUD counselor, and possess the resources to implement their counseling plan.

We offer multiple funding opportunities through HUD and other national funds to support our affiliates in delivering essential housing services.
